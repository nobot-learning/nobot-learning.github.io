Workshop Theme

NoBot Learning rethinks how we train robots. Can we develop robust robotic behaviors without touching physical robots? This workshop explores data-efficient, simulation-native, and human-centric training paradigms—leveraging AR/VR, human demonstration, and synthetic data to scale learning beyond the lab.

Topics of Interest

  • Human motion and teleoperation datasets
  • Sim-to-real transfer and sim-native learning
  • AR/VR for demonstration and annotation
  • Training with synthetic and crowd-sourced data
  • Hardware-free robot behavior supervision
  • Foundation models for robotics
  • Open benchmarks for robot-free learning
